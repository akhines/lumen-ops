Real Estate · AI Underwriting
Replaced hours of manual deal analysis with a 30-second workflow
The problem: A wholesaling company analyzing 20+ properties per day was spending 45 minutes per deal pulling comps, calculating ARV, estimating repairs, and building offers — all in spreadsheets.

What we built: An AI-powered underwriting platform that pulls live MLS comps, runs AVM blending across three valuation engines, generates smart offers, and builds negotiation plans — all from a single property address.

The result: Deal analysis dropped from 45 minutes to 30 seconds. The team now analyzes 3x more properties per day with higher accuracy than manual comps.
